Privacy Policy – Watch List Chrome Extension

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

The Watch List Chrome Extension is designed to help users store and manage a simple list of items directly in their browser. Your privacy is important, and this extension is built so that your data stays completely on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.
All data you enter into the extension is saved only to your browser’s local storage using Chrome Storage API (chrome.storage.local).

3. How Your Data Is Used

The extension only uses the data you provide (your watch list items) to display and persist them inside the extension interface. No data leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, external APIs, tracking scripts, or external databases.

5. Permissions

The extension uses the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage – Required to save your watch list to your device so it’s available the next time you open Chrome.

This extension does not request any additional permissions.

6. Data Sharing

We share no data with anyone. We cannot access your data. We do not upload data to any server.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the extension changes in a way that affects user privacy, this policy will be updated.
